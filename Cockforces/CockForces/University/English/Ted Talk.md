# 🛡️ TEDTalk – The Silent Battlefield: AI and Cybersecurity

---

## 📝 Versión Completa (detalle)

### 1. Title
**The Silent Battlefield: AI and Cybersecurity**

---

### 2. Introduction (Hook + Context)

**Hook (Slide 1)**  
"Imagine this: you wake up, your bank account is empty.  
The hospital near you cannot open patient files.  
The traffic lights in your city stop.  
This is not  fiction. This is a cyberattack"

**Introduce the topic**  
"Today I will talk about Artificial Intelligence in cybersecurity—  
the hidden fight that shapes our lives every day."

**Purpose**  
"My goal is to explain how AI can be both a danger and a solution,  
and why this affects not only companies or governments,  
but also you and me."

**Thesis**  
"AI in cybersecurity is not only for experts.  
It is a shared duty.  
In the digital world, it is a new human right."

---

### 3. Personal Connection (Slide 3 – puzzle)

"When I first learned programming, I thought it was only about solving puzzles.  
But later I saw: every line of code can be used by hackers—or even by AI systems.  
That is when I understood: protecting systems means protecting people.  

And here, **we are the missing piece.**"

---

### 4. Main Idea 1 – The Danger (Slide 4 – stats)

**Point**  
"Cyberattacks are growing fast. With AI, they are stronger and faster."

**Evidence**  
- In 2023, cybercrime cost over **11.5 trillion dollars** worldwide.  
- Every **39 seconds**, a cyberattack happens somewhere in the world.  
- **30%** of phishing attacks in 2023 were made with AI.  

**Example**  
"In 2021, a ransomware attack stopped the biggest fuel pipeline in the U.S.  
Now imagine this, but faster and smarter with AI."

---

### 5. Main Idea 2 – The Challenge (Slide 2 – human vs AI)

**Point**  
"Many people think cybersecurity is only for IT workers.  
But today, anyone can download AI tools online.  
Even a normal person with little knowledge can use AI to hack passwords or write dangerous code."

**Message**  
"This means we can be the weak point… or the strong defense.  
**AI can attack. But AI can also defend.**"

---

### 6. Main Idea 3 – The Solution / Future (Slide 5)

**Point**  
"The future of AI in cybersecurity depends on three things:"

1. **Education** – teaching digital skills in schools.  
2. **Strong systems** – building safe and secure platforms.  
3. **Cooperation** – countries working together with rules for AI.  

**Message**  
"From students to presidents, everyone has a role in this new battlefield."

---

### 7. Conclusion (Slide 6 – shield/lock image)

**Summary**  
"AI makes cybersecurity more dangerous, but also more powerful.  
If we learn and act, we can stop problems before they start."

**Closing idea**  
"This is a war without guns, but with AI on both sides.  
The result is still in our hands.  

**AI is the weapon.  
We are the shield.**"
